Overview

Goal: Kill the Templar.

In this encounter, you will be attacking the templar boss. There is a new mechanic called the relic, which is a shield that will spawn in the middle of the encounter. There will be a little bit of ad clear, then oracles will spawn. Oracles will spawn in sets of 3 for this encounter. You must once again shoot them in the specific order that they spawn in. During the oracle phase, the relic holder will be charging their super bar. After the 3 oracles are destroyed, the relic holder uses their super to break the Templarâ€™s shield, which starts the DPS phase. Rinse and repeat until the templar is defeated.

Static Challenge: Do not cancel the Templar's teleports.

Relic Holder Attacks

  • Horizontal Slam
    1. Hit the light attack key for your sword (Default is Left Click)
  • Ground Pound
    1. Jump into the air
    2. While mid-air hit the heavy attack button for your sword (Default is Right Click)
  • Guard
    1. Hit the block key for your sword (Default is c)
    • Note: It will drain the energy bar in the grenade slot.
  • Super Ability
    1. Once your super is charged, hit your super key. (Default is F)

Tips/Tricks/Loadout Recommendations

Normal Mode

  • Call the oracles that spawn by position in order.
    • If L3, L1, then R3 spawn, the first call should be L3, then the second call should be L1, then third call should be R3.
  • Do damage near the spawn area on the ledges.
  • Relic Holder can break an oracle by doing a ground pound attack (See Above).
  • Well of Radiance with lunafaction boots are very nice here for double slug shotguns.
  • During DPS phase, the relic holder should run towards the glowing beams that have a circle on the ground. Standing in the circles for a short amount of time will cancel the Templar's teleport, which extends your DPS phase.

Static Challenge

  • High burst DPS is the king here since you cannot cancel the Templar's teleports.
    • Hunters with the Celestial Nighthawk exotic and bottom tree gunslinger.
    • Titans with thundercrash and the exotic, Cuirass of the Falling Star.
    • Warlocks with top tree Nova Bomb
    • Double slug shotguns + anarchy is a good combination here
