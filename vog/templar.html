Overview

Goal: Kill the Templar.

In this encounter, you will be attacking the templar boss. There is a new mechanic called the relic, which is a shield that will spawn in the middle of the encounter. There will be a little bit of ad clear, then oracles will spawn. Oracles will spawn in sets of 3 for this encounter. You must once again shoot them in the specific order that they spawn in. During the oracle phase, the relic holder will be charging their super bar. After the 3 oracles are destroyed, the relic holder uses their super to break the Templarâ€™s shield, which starts the DPS phase. Rinse and repeat until the templar is defeated. During the DPS phase, whoever has the relic has to go to the beacons that will spawn around the middle to cancel the templar's teleport, which extends the damage phase.

Static Challenge: Do not cancel the Templar's teleports.

Rotating Challenge: Do not let the Templar teleport.

Master Mode: There are overload minotaurs and harpies will have arc shields. Completing the rotating challenge in master mode rewards Fatebringer (Timelost).

Relic Holder Attacks

  • Horizontal Slam (Similar to Titan Shield Bash except you don't need to run)
    1. Hit the light attack key for your sword (Default is Left Click)
  • Ground Pound
    1. Jump into the air
    2. While mid-air hit the heavy attack button for your sword (Default is Right Click)
  • Guard
    1. Hit the block key for your sword (Default is c)
    • Note: It will drain the energy bar in the grenade slot.
  • Super Ability
    1. Once your super is charged, hit your super key. (Default is F)

Tips/Tricks/Loadout Recommendations

Normal Mode

  • Call the oracles that spawn by position in order.
    • If L3, L1, then R3 spawn, the first call should be L3, then the second call should be L1, then third call should be R3.
  • Do damage near the spawn area on the ledges.
  • Relic Holder can break an oracle by doing a ground pound attack (See Above).
  • Well of Radiance with single cast supers for dps work nicely here.
  • For the static challenge, high burst DPS is the king here since you cannot cancel the Templar's teleports.
    • Hunters with the Celestial Nighthawk exotic and bottom tree gunslinger.
    • Titans with thundercrash and the exotic, Cuirass of the Falling Star.
    • Warlocks with top tree Nova Bomb

Master Mode

  • Try your best to clear ads and just power through in a single dps phase. As of season 18, linears with div and double specials provide the best total damage output.
  • Relic holder can do a little damage after canceling a teleport by dropping the relic, and then firing something like anarchy, witherhoard, or an auto-loading rocket.
  • There is a cheese where everyone dies before starting the encounter. The relic holder stays alive in the middle and cleanses once the oracles break. Then from there everyone respawns (since everone was dead before the encounter started, they can self-res) and powers through in one dps phase (keep going if the oracles come back up it should be fine).
