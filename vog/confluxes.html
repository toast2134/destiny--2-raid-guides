Goal: Defend the confluxes from the vex.

In this encounter, you will be defending confluxes that can spawn in 3 different locations from the vex that will try to sacrifice once they get near it. There will be 3 waves with confluxes spawning in different set locations as indicated in the map below. After a wave is cleared, the confluxes will go away, and the next wave of confluxes will spawn after a short period of time. The first wave of confluxes will spawn in the middle. The second wave of confluxes will spawn on the left and right sides. The third and last wave of confluxes will spawn in all 3 locations. Once the third wave is cleared, the encounter will be over.

Static Challenge: Destroy the wyverns with supers. (must be final blows)

Tips/Tricks/Loadout Recommendations

Normal Mode

  • There will be Overload Minotaurs that will spawn periodically. Make sure you kill these guys.
  • It is recommended to assign 2 people per side as ads will still keep spawning even if a conflux isn't there.
  • Prioritize the Wyverns as those are the ones with the most health and will do the most damage.
  • Beware of the fanatics. They will drop pools which will mark you for negation and will kill you if you don't cleanse in the middle plate.
    • It is advised to make sure that no one else has been marked for negation before you cleanse so that you and your teammate can cleanse at the same time.
    • To cleanse, there is a plate in the middle that you must run through at relatively the same time as your teammates if they are also marked for negation.
  • Anything that can kill red bar enemies is useful here.
  • Once again, grenade launchers with blinding grenades are very useful here.
  • One time use supers are amazing here, such as blade barrage for hunters or thundercrash for titans.
  • Chaos Reach works really well for warlocks as well.

Static Challenge

  • The most effective way to kill wyverns with super is to bring them down to low health with your weapons then to kill them with your super.
  • The final blow MUST be from your super.
  • Otherwise the encounter is the same.
