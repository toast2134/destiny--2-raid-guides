Overview

Goal: Defend the confluxes from the vex.

In this encounter, you will be defending confluxes that can spawn in 3 different locations from the vex that will try to sacrifice once they get near it (as indicated when the pillar glows red). There will be 3 waves with confluxes spawning in different set locations as indicated in the map below. After a wave is cleared, the confluxes will go away, and the next wave of confluxes will spawn after a short period of time. The first wave of confluxes will spawn in the middle. The second wave of confluxes will spawn on the left and right sides. The third and last wave of confluxes will spawn in all 3 locations. Once the third wave is cleared, the encounter will be over.

This encounter technically introduces the "Marked for negation" mechanic. During your waves of ads, vex fanatics (the ones that explode) will occasionally spawn. When they die, they will drop a pool of vex juice which will give u a debuff called "Marked for negation" if you step in it. If you leave this alone, you will eventually die. To get rid of the debuff, run through the pool in the middle.

Static Challenge: Destroy the wyverns with supers (must be final blows).

Rotating Challenge: Destroy the wyvern as they are sacrificing (they will glow red)

Master mode: There will be barrier hob-goblins that spawn on left and right side (from where the other ads come out. The harpies will have arc shields so arc weapons are amazing for master mode. There are still overload minotaurs.
Completing the rotating challenge in master mode awards Vision

Tips/Tricks/Loadout Recommendations

Normal Mode

  • There will be Overload Minotaurs that will spawn periodically. Make sure you kill these guys.
  • It is recommended to assign 2 people per side as ads will still keep spawning even if a conflux isn't there.
  • Prioritize the Wyverns as those are the ones with the most health and will do the most damage.
  • Beware of the fanatics.
    • It is advised to make sure that no one else has been marked for negation before you cleanse so that you and your teammate can cleanse at the same time.
    • To cleanse, there is a plate in the middle that you must run through at relatively the same time as your teammates if they are also marked for negation.
  • Anything that can kill red bar enemies is useful here.
  • Once again, grenade launchers with blinding grenades are very useful here.
  • Single cast supers are amazing here, such as Blade Barrage / Gathering Storm for hunters or thundercrash for titans.
  • Nova bomb / Chaos reach works really well for warlocks.
