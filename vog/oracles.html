Overview

Goal: Shoot the oracles in the correct order.

In this encounter there will be oracles that are glowing white boxes that will spawn throughout the map in a certain order. They will spawn in 7 different locations in 5 different waves. The first wave will have 3 oracles. The second wave will have 4 oracles. The third wave will have 5 oracles. The fourth wave will have 6 oracles. The fifth and final wave will have all 7 oracles spawn. You must shoot the oracles in the correct order that they appear in. They will reveal the pattern TWICE before you can shoot them for each wave. Once the fifth wave is cleared, the encounter is over. If you

Static Challenge: Do not kill the hob-goblins on the side of the arena.

Rotating Challenge: Each person cannot shoot the same oracle.

Master Mode: There are overload minotaurs that occasionally spawn. Completing the rotating challenge in master mode rewards Praedyth's Revenge (Timelost).

Tips/Tricks/Loadout Recommendations

Normal Mode

  • It is heavily recommended to assign everyone to an oracle.
  • You have to pair one of the oracles up. The easiest combinations would be either L1 + L2, L1 + middle, or R1 + R2.
  • Call out the oracles in the order that they spawn.
    • For instance, if L3, R2, then Middle oracles spawn. The person at L3 calls 1, the person at R2 calls 2, and the person at Middle calls 3.
  • Xenophage can one shot the oracles.
  • Shotguns (like First In Last Out) are also really nice here if you don't have xenophage.
  • Anything long range such as a sniper rifle or scout rifle can deal with hob-goblins.
  • For the static challenge, standing behind cover is the easiest way to avoid the hob-goblins. However, you can also use a well of radiance or a titan bubble.
  • For the rotating challenge, it is easiest to have each person rotate clockwise each wave (whether they shoot or not). For example, whoever starts at L3, goes to R3, then R2, etc.

Master Mode

  • There is a spot where you can see all the oracles and unless you have previous experience you should only really stand here for protection against hob-goblins or just not at all (you really dont need it).
  • Blinding grenades are good here.
  • Any super is decent here, whether you prefer survivability to survive against the ads or single cast to one shot the overloads.
