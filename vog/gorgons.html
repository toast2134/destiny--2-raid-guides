Overview

Goal: Evade the gorgons.

In this encounter you will be sneaking around the gorgons to make it to the exit. In order to get the chest, you must shoot the floating vex cubes which are indicated in the map.

Static Challenge: Kill 2 gorgons within 3 seconds of each other.

Rotating Challenge: None.

Master Mode: No real differences here.

Tips/Tricks/Loadout Recommendations

Normal Mode

  • Void hunters are very good here.
    • Their melee is a throwable smoke that makes the whole team invisible (this is one of the aspects).
    • Use the exotic chestplate Omnioculus to get 2 charges.
    • If everyone is smoked, then the melee charge is refunded.
  • You can also jump super high between certain rocks to evade the gorgons.
  • There is also the "chad" strategy, where as denoted in the image above, where you simply hug the wall.
  • For the static challenge:
    1. Unlock the secret chest.
    2. Place a rally flag.
    3. Wait outside behind the rock until 2 gorgons get kind of close together.
    4. Unload everything you have (including supers).
    • Wardcliff Coil (with its catalyst) is very good against the gorgons as they will gain arc shields that will do extra damage when destroyed by wardcliff.
