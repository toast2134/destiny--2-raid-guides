Overview

Goal: Defend the spires in both portals.

In this encounter you will be defending confluxes, one inside of the left portal and one inside the right portal in the main room. The left portal is called Mars (since the terrain inside is like a desert) and the right portal is called Venus (since the terrain inside is more jungle-like, which is what Venus looked like in Destiny 1). Start by clearing the ads and killing the gatekeeper hydra. Have one person stand on each plate to open the portal on their respective side, their job is to defend this plate from the overload minotaurs that spawn and other ads. Send one person into each portal and have someone else that is outside of the portals (but not on the plate) pick up the relic. Inside each portal, there will be waves of vex that will try to sacrifice themselves just like in the confluxes encounter. As you defend the conflux, there will be shielded minotaurs that spawn. The shields must be broken by the relic holder. When the first minotaur spawns, call out which side and have the initial relic holder go into the corresponding portal. Whenever someone brings the relic through a portal, they will have a debuff that wonâ€™t let them go through another portal for 45 seconds called Teleport Destabilized. Once the relic holder kills the minotaur, they must swap weapons to drop the relic so that the other person can pick it up. Once the person inside the portal comes out, they will drop the relic so that someone else can take it to the opposing portal. (You are basically going back and forth between each side.) Repeat until 6 minotaurs are cleared. Then there will be a final stand in the center of the main room where you must defend the final conflux. Prioritize the shielded minotaurs and wyverns. After everything is dead, the encounter will be over.

Static Challenge: Kill the shielded minotaurs with the relic's super ability.

Rotating CHallenge: Kill the minotaur and wyvern that spawn each wave within 3 seconds of each other.

Master mode: Overload minotaurs still exist. Anti-barrier hob-goblins will spawn in during final phase.

Tips/Tricks/Loadout Recommendations

Normal Mode

  • There will be overload minotaurs.
  • Once you stand on the plate, you don't have to keep standing on it.
  • Kill gatekeepers (hydras) as soon as they spawn.
  • Blinding grenade launchers with anarchy are very nice for this encounter.
    • You can push back the minotaurs if you blind them (Just run into them).
  • You can freeze the minotaurs if the relic holder is falling behind by using stasis or by using Salvation's Grip
  • Wyverns will spawn so using burst supers such as blade barrage or thundercrash work well.
  • For the static challenge, freezing the minotaurs or pushing the minotaurs back with blinding grenade launchers is crucial here.
    • NOTE: The relic holder should NOT try to challenge the wyverns as they WILL one shot you.
    • The relic super USUALLY one shots the shielded minotaurs. If it doesn't for you, hit the minotaur with a single shield bash then use the super. Alternatively, jump into the air and use the super.
  • For the rotating challenge, just count down. The wyvern side should use Gjallarhorn or a super to burst it down. You should start killing as soon as relic holder breaks the minotaur shield (say go or time your countdown so that it lines up).

Master Mode

  • The challenge in master mode is pretty simple just be careful in staying alive and killing your ads beacuse they will be tankier.
  • Have seasonal mods to stun champions
  • Gjallarhorn works well for the wyverns if you don't have a super.
