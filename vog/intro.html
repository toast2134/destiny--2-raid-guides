Overview

Vault of Glass is a raid that was originally in Destiny 1 but was brought into Destiny 2 during Season of the Splicer (Season 14). The primary race are the vex, and the boss is "Atheon: Timeâ€™s Conflux". Because Destiny 1 and Destiny 2 are very different, Vault of Glass was slightly reworked to fit to the style of Destiny 2 raids. Destiny 2's Vault of Glass features a new vex enemy called a Wyvern and a new type of enemy called a Champion. In addition, there are slight differences in terms of mechanics for each encounter that will be explained later for returning Destiny 1 veterans to be aware of. There is also a master mode, which has more champions and requires higher light (usually pinnacle cap for the season +20 (from the artifact bonus) will be the recommended light).
