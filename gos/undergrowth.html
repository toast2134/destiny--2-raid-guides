Overview

Goal: Defend the relays to summon the consecrated mind.

This encounter introduces a new mechanic called the "Enlightened" Buff. Using the tether mechanic from last encounter, you tether from the floating boxes to the pillars. Once you tether, you will gain a buff called "Enlightened". Throughout the encounter, there will be vex with white shields that spawn. You must have the buff in order to shoot these shields off.

This encounter is relatively simple once you get a rhythm. There are 6 roles (one for everyone on the team). There are 4 relay defenders and 2 floaters. Illustrated in the image below, there are 4 relays. Each person must get to their relay to defend it. The 2 floaters will move between the relays to refresh people's "Enlightenment" buff. After everyone is at a relay, hydras called "Angelics" will spawn at one of the four relays (there will be 3, one for each lane). As you are progressing through Kill these, and after they spawn at each relay, the middle barriers will open up. There is a final relay in the middle where you do the same: tehter to get the buff, kill the angelics, and kill the ads with the shields. After 3 waves

Static Challenge: You can't tether if you have the Englightened buff.

Rotating Challenge: Everyone has to tether around the same time.
