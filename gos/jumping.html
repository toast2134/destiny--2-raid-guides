Overview

Goal: Get to the next encounter.

The jumping puzzle is fairly complex, but the route basically leads you to the secret chest.

  1. Hug the left side as you drop down (Go towards the platform that is moving up and down).
  2. Next to the platform, there is a hole in that hanging column. This is where the secret chest is.
    • Note: you can use the elevator, or if you know where the hole is, you can just jump straight into it.
    • The secret chest will only drop items that you've gotten already or raid mods if you don't have them.
  3. From there you go out the back, straight to the wall in front of you (there are platforms that are coming out of this wall).
    • If you cannot make it to the top, then there are leaves that you can land on that are slightly down to the right.
  4. If you made it to the top, go across to where the platforms are coming out of the other wall.
    • If you did not make it to the top, find the wall where the platforms are coming out (same wall as mentioned before). You want to jump between these two walls until you make it to the top.
  5. Once you've made it to the other wall, you can shoot the flower bulb to open it, jump onto it then go to where the light is. After that, the path is straight forward.

Tips/Tricks/Loadout Recommendations

  • Have something long-range to kill the harpies if they get annoying.
  • Movement exotics are good here (Titans use the Lion Rampant exotic boots).
  • Swords are good here (particularly swords with eager edge but a normal sword works fine, helps you see too).
