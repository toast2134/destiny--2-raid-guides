Overview

Garden of Salvation is a raid introduced during the Shadowkeep expansion. It was the first raid to introduce champions. It features the vex race and is relatively short compared to other raids. Garden of Salvation is also the first raid that was used for the pinnacle system, which kind of sucked at the time because there was no heavy weapon option from the raid.
